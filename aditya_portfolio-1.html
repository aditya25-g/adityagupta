Research & Consulting Projects

01 / 04
The Tannery Project — Leather Tannery Feasibility Study
180 Degrees Consulting, DSC Chapter  ·  Nov 2025–Present
Manufacturing Financial Modeling J&K Strategy

Full-stack feasibility study for establishing a leather tannery in Jammu & Kashmir — a region with no formal tanning cluster. Covered market sizing, product mix strategy, location analysis, route-to-market, regulatory compliance, and complete financial modeling.

  • ₹7.7 Cr capex model with 30% gross margin at ₹90/sq ft selling price
  • 6-parameter district scoring matrix across 7 J&K locations
  • Recommended Crust → Finished Leather over commodity Wet Blue
  • Analyzed DGFT Trade Notice 11/2024-25, EU REACH, LWG certification
  • Showed 8%→3% rejection reduction nearly doubles EBITDA
02 / 04
Project Lilypad — EV Two-Wheeler Marketplace Strategy
180 Degrees Consulting, DSC Chapter  ·  Nov 2025–Present
EV / Mobility Platform Strategy Startup Porter's 5 Forces

Strategic analysis of Lilypad — India's first dedicated multi-brand EV two-wheeler marketplace headquartered in Noida. Covered SWOT, competitive benchmarking, trust gap audit, fleet opportunity mapping, and growth lever framework.

  • EV penetration projected at ~35% of 2W sales by FY31 (CRISIL)
  • Live site audit identified broken CTAs & policy language eroding trust
  • Mapped Blinkit/Instamart/Zepto dark-store density for fleet anchoring
  • E2W TCO ~55% lower than ICE; EV: ₹0.6–1.0/km vs petrol ₹2.5–3.0/km
  • 4-lever growth strategy: Ownership Stack, Intelligence, Fleet, Expansion
03 / 04
EU–India Free Trade Agreement Research Report
Academic Group Project, Dyal Singh Evening College  ·  Jan 2026
International Trade Macroeconomics Policy Analysis

Comprehensive research report analyzing the EU–India FTA signed January 2026 — one of the most significant trade agreements in modern history — covering historical context, economic significance, sector-wise impact, and strategic implications for India.

  • EU is India's largest trading partner — €120B goods trade in 2024
  • FTA delivers market access for 99%+ of India's exports duty-free
  • EU FDI in India: €82.3B (2019) → €140.1B (2023), ~25% of India's total FDI
  • Framed FTA as India's strategic pivot toward Western supply chains
  • Mapped sector-wise impact: textiles, IT services, pharma, automotive, banking
04 / 04
BlueTea India — GreenPack Sustainability Strategy
TATVA Case Competition  ·  Lady Shri Ram College for Women  ·  2025
Sustainability D2C / E-commerce ESG Case Competition

Case competition submission for Blue Tea India proposing a phased transition from 100% plastic packaging to a sustainable stack — without compromising freshness, durability, or cost competitiveness across D2C and Amazon channels.

  • Material transitions across 6 packaging categories (Kraft+PLA, FSC glass, EN13432 mailers)
  • ROI break-even at ~14 months; 8–12% premium pricing potential unlocked
  • 3-phase GTM: D2C pilot → Amazon IN/Flipkart → Amazon US/UK
  • ~85% plastic volume reduction; full EPR compliance by month 15
  • Unlocks Amazon Climate Pledge Friendly badge & Flipkart Green Seller certification
